James "Gene" Odom

Mar 14, 2018

Today we meet in the Santo, Texas home of WWII veteran James "Gene" Odom. To avoid the imminent draft, Gene volunteered for the U.S Navy following his eighteenth birthday. He was soon after assigned to a supply ship, and deployed to the war-torn pacific. Over seventy years later, Gene shares the captivating details of Leyte and Okinawa for his first time on camera.

Two months after our interview, the Gary Sinise Foundation's "Soaring Valor" program hosted an all-expense-paid trip for Gene Odom and I.
